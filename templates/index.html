COVID-19 Deaths Classifier

No other websites gave insight into this data, that was considered
crucial during the initial outbreak of the Covid-19 Coronavirus.
Hospitals across New York were making thousands of life critical decisions using death metrics.
The Govt. of Karnataka uploads this very data Here.
This Webapp grabs the PDF files, extracts the data in them
and presents them in a coincise data charts and other visualizations.
